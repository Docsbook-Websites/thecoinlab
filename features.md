# Features

TheCoinLab is more than a deploy button - it includes the tooling a launch actually needs.

## Token controls

- **Mintable and burnable** - adjust supply after launch when your tokenomics call for it.
- **Tax engine** - configure buy and sell taxes.
- **Anti-whale limits** - cap per-wallet holdings to discourage concentration.
- **Pausable and blacklist** - pause transfers or block addresses when you need to.

## Distribution

- **Airdrop and multi-send** - distribute tokens to many wallets in one operation.
- **Presale module** - run a token offering before public listing.

## Liquidity

- **One-click DEX liquidity** - add a liquidity pool on a decentralized exchange without manual steps.

## Management

- **Dashboard** - handle minting, burning, airdrops, and liquidity from one place after launch.
- **AI-powered tokenomics assistance** - get help shaping supply and tax parameters.
