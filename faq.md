# Frequently asked questions

## Getting started

**What is TheCoinLab?**
A no-code token creation platform that deploys ERC-20, SPL (Solana), and BEP-20 tokens in under 60 seconds. You configure the token through the interface; the platform handles the smart contract deployment.

**Do I need to know how to code?**
No. You configure everything in a form - no Solidity and no command line.

**Which blockchains are supported?**
12 networks, including Ethereum (ERC-20), Solana (SPL), BNB Chain (BEP-20), and Polygon. See [supported chains](token-standards/supported-chains.md).

**How long does deployment take?**
Under 60 seconds once you confirm and pay.

**Do I own the token after deployment?**
Yes. The owner wallet holds the token's admin rights - see [ownership and anti-rug](security/ownership-and-anti-rug.md).

## Payments and pricing

**How much does it cost?**
One-time pricing per tier, starting at EUR 19 - see [pricing](pricing.md).

**Can I pay with crypto?**
Yes, crypto or card.

**Are there gas fees on top of the platform fee?**
Yes. Network gas fees on the underlying chain are separate from the platform fee.

## Safety and technical

**Are the smart contracts audited?**
Yes - deployments use pre-audited, explorer-verified contract templates. See [audits](security/audits.md).

**Can I verify my contract on Etherscan?**
Yes. Contracts are verified on their block explorer with a transparency badge.

**Do you store my private keys?**
No. You deploy and manage with your own wallet - see [wallet integration](wallet-integration.md).

**What token standard does TheCoinLab use for Solana?**
SPL, including SPL Token 2022 extensions where applicable.
