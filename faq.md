# Frequently asked questions

## Do I need to know Solidity or use a CLI?

No. You configure the token in a form and the platform deploys a pre-audited contract for you - no Solidity and no command line.

## Which chains and standards are supported?

12 blockchains, including Ethereum (ERC-20), Solana (SPL), BNB Chain (BEP-20), and Polygon.

## Are the contracts audited?

The platform deploys pre-audited, explorer-verified contracts and includes a transparency badge so holders can verify the contract on the block explorer.

## How do I pay?

A single payment by crypto or card triggers deployment. Pricing is one-time per tier, with no subscription - see [Pricing](pricing.md).

## What can I do after launch?

From the dashboard you can mint, burn, run airdrops and multi-sends, and add DEX liquidity with one click.

## Can I run a presale?

Yes, the presale module is available on the Launch tier - see [Features](features.md).
