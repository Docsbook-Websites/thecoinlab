# Wallet integration

You deploy and manage tokens with the wallet for your target chain. TheCoinLab supports the standard wallets on Solana and EVM networks.

## Solana

- **Phantom** - browser extension and mobile wallet; the most popular Solana wallet.
- **Solflare** - browser extension and web wallet; supports hardware wallets.

## EVM (Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, and L2s)

- **MetaMask** - the standard EVM wallet. Supports Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, and all L2s.

## Choosing

Match the wallet to the chain you chose in [getting started](getting-started.md): Phantom or Solflare for Solana, MetaMask for any EVM chain.
