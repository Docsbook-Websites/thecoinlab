# How TheCoinLab works

Deploying a token normally means writing a smart contract, testing it, and pushing it on-chain from a command line. TheCoinLab replaces that with a configuration step and a single payment - the platform compiles and deploys a verified contract on the chain you choose.

## Chains and standards

You choose the network, and TheCoinLab uses the matching token standard:

- **Ethereum** - ERC-20
- **Solana** - SPL
- **BNB Chain** - BEP-20
- **Polygon** - and more, across 12 supported networks

## Pre-audited contracts

Rather than deploying untested code, the platform ships pre-audited, explorer-verified contracts. Verification and a transparency badge are included so holders can confirm the contract on the block explorer.

## What the platform handles

You configure; the platform deploys. That covers contract compilation, deployment to your chosen chain, explorer verification, and the launch tooling described in [Features](features.md) - so you never touch Solidity or a CLI.
