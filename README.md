# Launch a token in 60 seconds

TheCoinLab lets you configure and deploy a crypto token across 12 blockchains without writing Solidity, touching a CLI, or hiring a developer. You pick a chain and features, pay once, and the platform deploys a pre-audited, explorer-verified contract for you.

## What you get

- **No-code deployment** - configure name, supply, and features in a form; the contract ships for you.
- **Multi-chain from one interface** - Ethereum (ERC-20), Solana (SPL), BNB Chain (BEP-20), Polygon, and more.
- **Pre-audited contracts** - verified smart contracts with explorer verification and a transparency badge included.
- **A full launch suite** - tax engine, anti-whale limits, airdrops, presale, and one-click DEX liquidity.
