# How to add liquidity after deploy

A token is only tradable once it has a liquidity pool. TheCoinLab adds liquidity to a decentralized exchange in one click from the dashboard.

## What liquidity is

A liquidity pool pairs your token with a base asset (like ETH, SOL, or a stablecoin) so people can buy and sell it. When you add liquidity you receive LP tokens representing your share of the pool.

## Steps

1. Open your token in the dashboard after deployment.
2. Choose the DEX and the base asset for the pair.
3. Set how much of your token and the base asset to seed the pool with.
4. Confirm - TheCoinLab handles the pool creation and liquidity add in one step.

## Lock your liquidity

Holders trust tokens whose liquidity is locked, because it prevents the owner from pulling the pool. See [liquidity locking](../security/liquidity-locking.md) for how and why.
