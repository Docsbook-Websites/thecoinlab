# Getting started

A token launch is three steps: configure, deploy, manage.

## 1. Configure

Select your blockchain, set the token name and total supply, and choose the features you want - mintable, burnable, a tax engine, anti-whale limits, and more. Everything is a no-code choice in the interface.

## 2. Pay and deploy

A single payment - by crypto or card - triggers deployment of your pre-audited smart contract. The contract goes on-chain and is verified on the block explorer.

**Expected result:** a live, explorer-verified token on your chosen chain, deployed in under a minute, with no contract code written by you.

## 3. Manage and grow

From the dashboard you can mint, burn, run airdrops and multi-sends, and add liquidity to a DEX with one click. See the full toolset in [Features](features.md).
