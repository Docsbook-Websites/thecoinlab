# Create your first token

A token launch is three steps: configure, deploy, manage. You will have a live, explorer-verified token in under a minute.

## Prerequisites

- A wallet for your target chain - [Phantom or Solflare](wallet-integration.md) for Solana, [MetaMask](wallet-integration.md) for Ethereum, BNB Chain, or Polygon.
- A payment method - crypto or card.

## 1. Configure

Select your blockchain, set the token name and total supply, and choose the features you want - mintable, burnable, a tax engine, anti-whale limits, and more. Every option is a no-code choice in the interface. See [token features](guides/token-features.md) to decide what to enable.

## 2. Pay and deploy

A single payment - by crypto or card - triggers deployment of your pre-audited smart contract. The contract goes on-chain and is verified on the block explorer.

**Expected result:** a live, explorer-verified token on your chosen chain, deployed in under a minute, with no contract code written by you.

## 3. Manage and grow

From the dashboard you can mint, burn, run airdrops and multi-sends, and add liquidity to a DEX with one click. Adding liquidity is covered in [add liquidity after deploy](guides/add-liquidity.md).
