# Supported chains and standards

TheCoinLab deploys across 12 blockchain networks. You pick the chain; the platform uses the matching standard.

| Chain | Standard | Typical use |
|---|---|---|
| Ethereum | ERC-20 | DeFi credibility, deep liquidity |
| Solana | SPL / SPL Token 2022 | Low-fee, high-volume, meme coins |
| BNB Chain | BEP-20 | Low fees, large retail audience |
| Polygon | ERC-20 (EVM) | EVM tooling without Ethereum gas |

Additional networks are available in the deployment interface, bringing the total to 12 supported chains.

## SPL Token 2022

On Solana, TheCoinLab can use SPL Token 2022 extensions, which add capabilities such as transfer fees and richer metadata directly at the token-standard level.
